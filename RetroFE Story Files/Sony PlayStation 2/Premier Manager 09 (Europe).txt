Premier Manager 09 is the longest running football management simulation in the games industry.  The main focus of Premier Manager games lately is ease of use, aiming for a pick up and play audience rather than hard-core football simulation fanatics.  It offers a simpler management game with a less complex match and tactics engine to appeal to younger gamers who are looking for a casual introduction to football management.
