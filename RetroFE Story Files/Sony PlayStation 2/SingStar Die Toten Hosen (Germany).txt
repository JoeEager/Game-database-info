In time for the band's 25th anniversary, legendary German punk rockers Die Toten Hosen get their own edition of Sony's popular karaoke series. Featured are 24 of their most well-known (and party-compatible) tracks:  1. Eisgekühlter Bommerlunder 2. Hier kommt Alex 3. All die ganzen Jahre 4. Wünsch DIR was 5. Paradies 6. Bonnie
